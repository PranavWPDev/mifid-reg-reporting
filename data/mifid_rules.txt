MiFID II Field Mapping and Validation Rules

----------------------------------------
FIELD MAPPINGS
----------------------------------------

Field: trade_id
Synonyms: transaction_id, ref_no, trade_ref, trn_id, order_id
MiFID Field: Field 2 - Transaction Reference Number

Field: trade_datetime
Synonyms: trade_date, execution_time, trade_time, timestamp, exec_datetime
MiFID Field: Field 8 - Trading Date Time
Format: ISO 8601 (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS.sssZ)

Field: isin
Synonyms: instrument_id, security_id, instrument_code, security_code, product_id
MiFID Field: Field 9 - Instrument Identification Code
Format: 12-character ISIN (ISO 6166)

Field: executing_entity_lei
Synonyms: firm_lei, executing_firm, broker_lei, entity_id, reporting_firm
MiFID Field: Field 4 - Executing Entity Identification Code
Format: 20-character LEI (ISO 17442)

Field: buyer_lei
Synonyms: buyer_id, buy_side_lei, counterparty_buy, purchaser_lei, client_buy_lei
MiFID Field: Field 6 - Buyer Identification Code

Field: seller_lei
Synonyms: seller_id, sell_side_lei, counterparty_sell, vendor_lei, client_sell_lei
MiFID Field: Field 7 - Seller Identification Code

Field: price
Synonyms: trade_price, execution_price, deal_price, unit_price, fill_price
MiFID Field: Field 10 - Price
Rule: Must be positive numeric (> 0)

Field: currency
Synonyms: ccy, trade_ccy, settlement_ccy, price_currency, instrument_currency
MiFID Field: Field 11 - Currency
Rule: Must follow ISO 4217 (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF)

Field: quantity
Synonyms: qty, volume, shares, units, size, trade_size, lot_size
MiFID Field: Field 12 - Quantity
Rule: Must be positive numeric (> 0)

Field: venue
Synonyms: mic, exchange, market, trading_venue, execution_venue, platform
MiFID Field: Field 13 - Venue
Rule: Must follow ISO 10383 MIC code (XLON, XPAR, XETR, XNAS, etc.)

Field: notional_amount
Synonyms: notional, trade_value, gross_value, consideration, total_value
MiFID Field: Field 14 - Notional Amount
Rule: price × quantity

Field: report_status
Synonyms: status, msg_type, action_type, record_type, report_type
MiFID Field: Field 1 - Report Status
Valid Values: NEWT, AMND, CANC

Field: instrument_type
Synonyms: asset_class, product_type, security_type, instrument_class
MiFID Field: Instrument Classification
Valid Values: EQUITY, BOND, DERIVATIVE, ETF, FUND


----------------------------------------
VALIDATION RULES
----------------------------------------

BR-001:
notional_amount must equal price × quantity within 0.01 tolerance
Severity: CRITICAL

BR-002:
executing_entity_lei must be 20 alphanumeric characters (ISO 17442)
Severity: CRITICAL

BR-003:
buyer_lei and seller_lei must not be equal (no wash trades)
Severity: HIGH

BR-004:
price must be strictly positive (> 0)
Severity: HIGH

BR-005:
quantity must be strictly positive (> 0)
Severity: HIGH

BR-006:
trade_datetime must be valid ISO 8601 UTC format
Severity: CRITICAL

BR-007:
ISIN must follow ISO 6166 (12 characters with valid country prefix)
Severity: CRITICAL

BR-008:
currency must be valid ISO 4217 code
Severity: HIGH

BR-009:
venue must be valid ISO 10383 MIC code
Severity: HIGH


----------------------------------------
COMPLIANCE RULES
----------------------------------------

BR-010:
report_status must be NEWT, AMND, or CANC
Severity: CRITICAL

BR-011:
buyer_lei and seller_lei are mandatory for equity trades
Severity: HIGH

BR-012:
trade must be reported within T+1 (1 business day)
Severity: CRITICAL
